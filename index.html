About Me

Hi, I'm Milimo K. Mukkuli, a passionate web developer currently studying at the University of Zambia. My journey into web development began when I discovered how technology can solve real-world problems and connect people across the globe. I specialize in creating responsive, accessible, and user-friendly websites that deliver exceptional experiences.

Throughout my studies in CSC4035 Web Programming and Technologies, I've developed strong skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and following best practices. When I'm not coding, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects and mentoring fellow students who are starting their web development journey.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer and create web applications that make a positive impact. I'm particularly interested in responsive design, web accessibility, and performance optimization. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenging projects that push my boundaries.
